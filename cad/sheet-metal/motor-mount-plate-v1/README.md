# Motor Mount Plate v1 (Flat Plate Draft)

## Purpose
Adjustable motor mount plate for 6374-class BLDC on the single-ball 3-wheel launcher.

## Key dimensions
- Plate overall: 180 mm x 140 mm x 8 mm
- Center shaft clearance hole: 24 mm
- Motor mounting holes: 4 x 5.5 mm
- Hole pattern: configurable in SCAD, default square pattern around center
- Frame mounting holes: 4 x 6.0 mm
- Adjustment slot: 180 mm x 20 mm slot strip region (for 30~40 mm effective adjustment travel depending on bracket)

## Material
- 6061 aluminum plate, 8 mm preferred for P0

## Notes
- Final motor-hole spacing must be checked against the actual purchased motor drawing.
- This draft prioritizes adjustability and compatibility, not weight reduction.
