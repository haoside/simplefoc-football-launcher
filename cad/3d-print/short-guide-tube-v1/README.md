# Short Guide Tube v1 (3D Print Draft)

## Purpose
Short detachable guide tube for single-ball 3-wheel launcher.
Used only for short guidance / initial direction stabilization.
Not a feed tube, not a long barrel.

## Key dimensions
- Length: 200 mm
- Inner diameter: 232 mm
- Wall thickness: 4 mm
- Entry flare length: 35 mm
- Outer flange diameter: 270 mm
- Mount holes: 6 x M5 on PCD 252 mm

## Print suggestion
- Material: PETG / Nylon preferred
- Layer height: 0.24 mm
- Perimeters: 5+
- Infill: 35%+
- Print in 2 half-shells, then bolt together on flange if printer volume is limited

## Notes
- Keep inner wall smooth
- Do not let inner wall rub ball significantly
- Designed for A/B test against no-guide / 150mm / 250mm variants
