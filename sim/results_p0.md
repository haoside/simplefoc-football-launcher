## Interpretation

- `straight_pass` is the low-risk baseline preset.
- `light_curve_assist` uses moderate RPM differential and should be the first curve-ball field test.
- `standard_curve_assist` has higher RPM spread and should be tested only after straight/light presets are stable.

## Engineering cautions

- This is a screening model; real launch speed will depend strongly on wheel material, preload, contact time, and ball condition.
- Do not use these values for high-speed firing before protective cover and e-stop validation.
- Validate current draw with no ball first, then with low preload, then with real ball.
