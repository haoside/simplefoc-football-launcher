# P0 Assembly Model Notes v4

## Change summary

This revision adds more physical details to the assembly placeholder:

- Chassis cross rails.
- Top-plane diagonal braces.
- Rear pneumatic wheel bracket plates.
- Front caster bracket plates.
- Low battery tray with side retainers.
- Motor plate gussets behind the three wheel modules.
- Guide tube flange fastener pads.

## Intent

Make the OpenSCAD assembly look and behave more like a real prototype frame, not just a block layout.

## Still placeholder

- Fasteners are visual only.
- Wheel brackets still require real axle/hub dimensions.
- Battery tray dimensions require the selected battery pack drawing.
- Motor gussets require FEA / stiffness review before fabrication.
- PC guard cover is still an envelope, not a flat pattern.
